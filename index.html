hi, you've reached Su Doga's page 👾

I am an incoming PhD student at the Harvard University We-Search Lab, led by Prof. Ashwini Ashokkumar.

Before, I completed a pre-doc research position at the Stanford Social Neuroscience Lab, mentored by Prof. Jamil Zaki and Dr. Rui Pei, working on the Stanford Communities Project.

I received my B.S. in Data Science (with Honors) and B.A. in Psychology (with Honors) from The University of Chicago, advised by Prof. Yuan Chang Leong and working with Ren Calabro at the Computational Affective and Social Neuroscience Lab and the Data Science Institute.

My research examines how individuals choose to convey information with a communicative intent. I am particularly interested in how these intentional acts of narrative construction aggregate into broader social phenomena (with the help of global information networks), shaping collective norms and discourse.
